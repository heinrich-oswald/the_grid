Multiplier Event FAQ

👤 Who can participate?
Individual participants aged 17 and under with parental consent. You can join any round, even if you participated in previous rounds.
📅 What is the timeline?
Bi-weekly rounds with one week development time each. Every 3 months, the competition resets to 10 participants to prevent overcrowding.
📤 How to submit?
Submit a GitHub repo link and a 2-minute demo video before the deadline. Follow the submission template in the Discord #submissions channel.
📊 How are projects judged?
Innovation 30% • Functionality 30% • Design 20% • Presentation 20% — judges will post scores and short notes.
🏆 When will winners be announced?
Winners will be announced within 48 hours after the judging period concludes, with payments processed immediately.
💰 How does the entry fee work?
The ₹100 entry fee from each participant contributes to the total prize pool. This creates a high-stakes environment where everyone has skin in the game.
