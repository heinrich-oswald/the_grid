FAQ

Who can participate?
Anyone with a Discord account and the drive to ship. Solo or teams up to 3.
How to submit?
Submit a GitHub repo link and a 2-minute demo video before the deadline. Follow the submission template in the Discord #submissions channel.
How are projects judged?
Innovation 30% • Functionality 30% • Design 20% • Presentation 20% — judges will post scores and short notes.
What if slots don’t fill?
Prize and slot doubling only happens when a round fully fills. If the round doesn't fill, we run it as a regular round and publish next steps publicly.
How are winners paid?
Winners are paid within 48 hours. We publish masked transaction receipts in the Proofs section and in the Discord #winners channel.

How scaling works

If current round fills all slots, next round doubles both the number of slots and the prize pool (slot growth is capped by realistic capacity — we will not promise rounds we cannot pay).

Week n: Slots = 10 × 2^(n-1)
Prize = Slots × ₹100 × 0.5
Organizer cut = Slots × ₹100 × 0.5
