The Grid — Official Rules & Judging

Read carefully. Violations can lead to disqualification. This is a rapid competitive event — fair play is enforced.

  1. Eligibility: Open to anyone with a Discord account. Participants must comply with local laws. One account per participant.
  2. Teams: Teams up to 3 members. If a member leaves mid-round, the remaining team may continue.
  3. Entry & Payment: Entry fee is ₹100 per person (pilot). Payment via UPI/Razorpay as announced. Save payment receipts — required for verification.
  4. Slots & Scaling: Rounds start with 10 slots. Once a round fills, next round slots/prize double. We will not promise rounds we cannot fund.
  5. Submission: Submit a GitHub repo link and a 2-minute demo video. Use the official submission template in Discord. Late submissions disqualify.
  6. Judging: Innovation 30% • Functionality 30% • Design 20% • Presentation 20%. Judges' decisions are final; scores and short notes will be published.
  7. Code Ownership & Licenses: Projects must respect copyrights. Use only assets you have rights to. You may open-source your project if you wish.
  8. Fraud & Cheating: No plagiarism, no automated entries, no payment scams. If fraud is detected, involved entries are disqualified and reported.
  9. Refunds: Refund policy: If we cancel a round, refunds will be processed within 7 days. Admins will post masked txn proof of refunds.
  10. Payouts & Proof: Winners paid within 48 hours. Wallet txns will be masked and screenshots posted to #winners and on the site in Proofs.
  11. Code of Conduct: Respect others. No hate speech, harassment, or illegal content. Infractions: warn → mute → ban.
  12. Contact & Support: For disputes email admin@thegrid.example. Appeals will be logged and reviewed by moderators.

By participating you agree to these rules.
