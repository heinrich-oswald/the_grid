Eligibility

  • Age Requirement: Participants must be at most 17 years old and have parental consent to participate.
  • Geographic Restrictions: The Grid is open to participants worldwide, unless restricted by local laws or regulations.
  • Multiple Entries: Each participant is allowed only one entry per challenge, unless otherwise specified.

General Guidelines

  • Original Work: All submissions must be the original work of the participant(s). Plagiarism will result in disqualification.
  • Code Quality: Submitted code should be well-commented, readable, and follow best practices for the chosen programming language.
  • Fair Play: No cheating, hacking, or any form of unfair advantage will be tolerated.
  • Submission Deadline: All submissions must be received by the specified deadline. Late submissions will not be accepted.
  • Judging: All submissions will be judged based on the criteria specified for each challenge.

Entry and Payment

  • Entry Fee: Each challenge may have an entry fee, which will be clearly stated on the challenge page.
  • Payment Methods: We accept various payment methods. Details will be provided during registration.
  • Refund Policy: Refunds will only be considered if requested at least 48 hours before the start of the challenge.

Code Ownership and Usage

  • Intellectual Property: Participants retain ownership of their code, but grant The Grid a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use, display, and modify their submissions for promotional and educational purposes.
  • Open Source: Participants are encouraged to make their code open source, but it is not required.
  • Privacy: Personal information will be handled in accordance with our privacy policy.

Fraud and Disqualification

  • Fraudulent Activity: Any attempt to manipulate the competition or payment system will result in immediate disqualification and may lead to legal action.
  • Disqualification: The Grid reserves the right to disqualify any participant at any time for violating the rules or engaging in unethical behavior.

Payouts

  • Prize Distribution: Prizes will be distributed within 7 days after the announcement of results.
  • Payout Methods: Prizes will be paid via the method specified by the winner, subject to verification.
  • Taxes: Winners are responsible for any taxes associated with their prizes.

Note: Specific rules for each challenge (Multiplier, Teams, Time Bound) are available on their respective rules pages. These general rules apply to all challenges, but the specific rules may contain additional details and requirements.

Stay Active: Make sure to stay active in the Discord server as all important announcements will be shared there.
