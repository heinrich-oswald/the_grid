TimeBound Challenge — Rules & Guidelines

Welcome to The Grid's TimeBound Challenge! This is a fast-paced coding competition where participants complete challenges within strict time limits. Below are the specific rules for this challenge.

Speed Focused

Complete coding challenges under time pressure to test your skills.

Varied Challenges

Multiple rounds with different types of coding problems to solve.

Real-time Competition

Compete against other participants in real-time leaderboards.

Eligibility

  • Age Requirement: Participants must be at most 17 years old and have parental consent to participate.
  • Experience Level: Open to all skill levels, from beginners to professionals.
  • Single Entry: Each participant is allowed only one entry per TimeBound event.

Challenge Structure

The TimeBound Challenge consists of weekly 2-hour coding sessions where participants will be given a prompt via the Discord server and must create something based on that prompt within the time limit.

Important: All prompts will be announced in the Discord server at the start of each session.

Entry and Payment

  • Entry Fee: ₹100 per participant per event.
  • Payment Methods: UPI/Razorpay as announced in the Discord server.
  • Payment Proof: Save payment receipts — required for verification.
  • No Limited Slots: TimeBound Challenge does not have limited slots.
  • Refund Policy: Refunds will only be considered if requested at least 48 hours before the start of the event.

Timeline

  • Registration Period: Open on an ongoing basis.
  • Event Frequency: Weekly 2-hour sessions.
  • Session Schedule: Each week's session has a fixed start time as announced in the Discord server.
  • Results Announcement: Winners will be announced immediately after the judging period concludes each week.

Submission Guidelines

  • Platform: All challenges will be completed on the official The Grid platform.
  • Automatic Submission: Code is automatically submitted when the time limit expires.
  • Testing: Real-time feedback is provided as you submit solutions.
  • No External Resources: Participants may not use external resources or AI tools during the challenge.

Scoring System

  • Points per Problem: Problems are assigned points based on difficulty.
  • Time Bonus: Points are awarded for solving problems quickly.
  • Accuracy: Points are deducted for incorrect submissions.
  • Leaderboard: Real-time leaderboards are updated throughout each round.

Judging Criteria

The TimeBound Challenge is judged based on the following criteria:

Final scores are calculated using a combination of these factors, with specific weightings depending on the challenge type.

Prizes and Payouts

  • Winners Selection: Winners will be selected based on the judging criteria for each week's challenge.
  • Prize Money: Prize money will be announced soon.
  • Payout Timeline: Winners will be paid within 48 hours after the announcement of results.
  • Payout Methods: Prizes will be paid via the method specified during registration.
  • Prize Proof: Wallet transactions will be masked and screenshots posted in the Discord server and on the website.

Fraud and Disqualification

  • Cheating: Any form of cheating, including but not limited to using external resources, AI tools, or collaborating with others, will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Payment Fraud: Any attempt to manipulate the payment system will result in immediate disqualification and may lead to legal action.
  • Multiple Accounts: Using multiple accounts to gain an unfair advantage is strictly prohibited.

Important: These rules are specific to the TimeBound Challenge. Please also review our General Rules and Code of Conduct, which apply to all our challenges.

Stay Active: Make sure to stay active in the Discord server as all important announcements will be shared there.
